# Public-Belief Skilled-Player Calibration Report

## Result

The pure public-belief contracts now distinguish deterministic public knowledge
from ambiguous hidden truth before v7/v7 materialization.

- Reliable known species + one legal format ability → deterministic
  `INFERRED` ability (Gholdengo → Good as Gold).
- Multiple legal abilities remain a possible set with unknown concrete ability.
- Unknown species or unresolved Illusion prevents species-derived collapse.
- Own legal-request ability, item, moves, and Tera type are exact `KNOWN` facts.
- Explicit item reveal/activation is `KNOWN`; deterministic public deduction is
  `INFERRED`; one absent probabilistic secondary is non-evidence.
- Speed ranges carry no exact value; exact speed requires a public/logical
  inference supplied explicitly.

## Effective context

The inferred singleton ability flows through the existing
`EffectiveAbilityContext`. Known Neutralizing Gas and Gastro Acid suppress it,
known Mold Breaker-style bypass ignores it, and known Ability Shield protects
it. Ambiguous ability and unknown item identities remain unavailable rather
than guessed. Known Safety Goggles blocks powder; unknown Safety Goggles does
not.

## Scope

This is a contract/test calibration, not a live-state rewrite. Full propagation
of Illusion identity state and species/format ability tables through live
extraction remains a separate integration task. No state/action schema changed,
and no dataset was materialized.
